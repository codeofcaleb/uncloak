# Lemma: Natural Properties of Divisibility
### Proposition 1
For $a,b,c\in \mathbb Z$, the following hold:
- $a\mid b \land b\mid c \implies a\mid c$
- $a\mid b \land b\mid a \implies a=\pm b$
- $a\mid b \land a\mid c\implies a\mid(b\pm c)$
